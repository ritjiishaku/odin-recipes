Beef Stir Fry with Vegetables

Description

This Beef Stir Fry with Vegetables is a quick, colorful, and healthy dish packed with flavor. Perfect for a weeknight dinner, it features tender strips of beef, crisp-tender vegetables, and a savory garlic-ginger sauce. This hearty stir fry pairs wonderfully with steamed rice or noodles, making it a satisfying and balanced meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, marinate the beef with 1 tablespoon soy sauce and 1 teaspoon cornstarch. Let it sit for 15–20 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the marinated beef to the pan and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes until browned. Once cooked, remove the beef and set it aside.
  4. Add the remaining tablespoon of oil to the pan. Sauté the minced garlic and grated ginger until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  5. Add the broccoli florets, sliced bell pepper, and julienned carrot to the pan. Stir-fry for 3–4 minutes until the vegetables are just tender but still crisp.
  6. Return the beef to the pan and add the remaining soy sauce and oyster sauce. Toss everything together to coat the beef and vegetables in the sauce.
  7. Serve the stir fry hot over steamed rice or noodles, and enjoy a colorful and flavorful dish!
